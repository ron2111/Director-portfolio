About Me

  • Rudraksh vashisht is a Director, Producer, and First Assistant Director based in Delhi. He started his journey in the world of filmmaking with corporate videos and then transitioned into the role of a 3rd Assistant Director. He is still on the rise, steadily climbing the ladder and nurturing his aspiration of becoming a storyteller. He’s also dedicated to empowering others to weave their own narratives.
  • Over the course of his journey, he’s had the privilege of serving as First AD on 200+ commercials in both Delhi and Mumbai. He is a passionate filmmaker who switches on his camera and captures stories as he experiences them. Currently, he strives to translate his filmmaking expertise to the world of commercials, equipped with his refined skill set and extensive professional knowledge.
  • He’s been awarded by the Chief Minister of Haryana and appreciated by the Ambassador of Ecuador for his exceptional documentary work.
  • He holds a Master’s degree in Filmmaking from Amity School of Film and Drama (Noida). He remains dedicated as a passionate Assistant Director in the industry.
